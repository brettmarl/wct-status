# CAMPFIRE BAN ENDS MAY 15 AT NOON
The West Coast Trail campfire ban remains in effect until May 15 at noon.

BC Wildfire has announced that Category 1 campfires will be permitted again across the Coastal Fire Centre starting Friday, May 15 at noon.

Until then, campfires remain prohibited on the West Coast Trail. Propane backpacking stoves are still allowed.

[More information](https://blog.gov.bc.ca/bcwildfire/campfires-permitted-across-coastal-fire-centre-due-to-decreased-fire-risk/)


# 2026 TRAIL OPEN 
The trail is open. First hiker-submitted report indicates dry conditions at the south end.
